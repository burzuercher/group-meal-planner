Privacy Policy for Group Menu Planner

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Group Menu Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Group Menu Planner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name: Required for identification within your groups
  • Profile Photo: Optional image to personalize your profile
  • Party Size: Number of adults and children in your group
  • Email and Password: Optional, only if you choose to link your account for cross-device access
  • Group Memberships: Groups you create or join
  • Menu Data: Meal proposals, menu items, reservations, and attendance status
  • Notification Preferences: Your settings for meal reminders

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous User ID: A unique identifier created automatically when you first use the app
  • Device Type: Information about your device for notification delivery
  • App Version: To provide appropriate features and support

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Contacts from your device
  • Financial information or payment details
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking cookies
  • Health information
  • Sensitive personal information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Coordinating meal planning within your groups
  • Displaying your profile to other group members
  • Sending meal reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Generating AI-illustrated menu images
  • Synchronizing your data across devices (if you link your account)
  • Improving app functionality and fixing bugs

4. How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) services:

  • Firebase Firestore: Structured data (groups, menus, items)
  • Firebase Authentication: Account credentials (if you link your account)
  • Firebase Storage: Images (profile photos, menu images)
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Secure server-side operations

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. How We Share Your Information

5.1 Within the App

Your name, profile photo, party size, and meal activity are visible to members of groups you join. This is essential for meal coordination.

5.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Data storage, authentication, and hosting (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Google Gemini AI: Menu image generation (only menu titles are sent, no personal information)

5.3 What We DO NOT Do

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Use your data for advertising or tracking purposes
  • Share your data across different groups you belong to
  • Provide your information to social media platforms

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all your information in the app
  • Correct Your Data: Edit your profile, party size, and preferences at any time
  • Export Your Data: Download all your data in JSON format (Profile → Export Data)
  • Delete Your Account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data (Profile → Delete Account)
  • Opt-Out of Notifications: Disable meal reminders in app settings or system settings
  • Leave Groups: Exit any group at any time

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as you have an active account. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile is permanently deleted from all groups
  • Your authentication credentials are removed
  • Your reservations and attendance records are deleted
  • Your profile photo is deleted from our servers
  • Your menu proposals are anonymized (changed to "Former Member")

Note: Menu proposals you created remain visible to maintain group history, but your name is replaced with "Former Member" to protect your privacy.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app allows tracking party sizes (including children count), but this is just a number for meal planning purposes and is not personally identifiable information about specific children.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at groupmenuplanner@gmail.com, and we will delete it.

9. Account Linking and Cross-Device Access

By default, your account is anonymous and tied to your device. If you choose to link your account with an email and password:

  • Your anonymous account is upgraded to a permanent account
  • You can sign in from any device using your email and password
  • Your data synchronizes across all devices where you sign in
  • Your email is stored securely and not shared with other users

10. AI-Generated Content

Menu images are generated using Google Gemini AI. When generating an image:

  • Only the menu title (e.g., "Thanksgiving Dinner") is sent to the AI
  • No personal information is included in AI requests
  • Generated images are stored in Firebase Storage
  • Images are visible to all members of the group

AI-generated images are clearly labeled in the app with "Image created with AI" text.

11. Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use local notifications (scheduled on your device) to remind you about upcoming meals. These notifications:

  • Are created and stored locally on your device
  • Are not sent through external servers
  • Do not track your behavior or location
  • Respect your quiet hours settings (default: 10 PM - 8 AM)
  • Can be disabled at any time in app settings or system settings

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will be changed
  • You will be notified through the app or via email (if you linked your account)
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Firebase servers, which may be located in various countries. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws.

Firebase complies with applicable data protection frameworks, including GDPR and CCPA.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: groupmenuplanner@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 7 business days

15. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation - European Union)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act - United States)
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • Apple App Store Privacy Guidelines

16. Your Consent

By using Group Menu Planner, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app and delete your account.
